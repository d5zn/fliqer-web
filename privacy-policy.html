Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2025

Your Privacy Matters
Fliqer is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use our app.

1. Overview

Fliqer ("we," "our," or "us") is a video frame extraction app for iOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Identifier (no real email collected): To create an account, the app generates a pseudonymous identifier in the form user_[UUID]@fliq.app. This is not your real email and is used only to operate your account and restore access across reinstalls/devices. We do not request or store your personal email unless you explicitly provide it when contacting support.
  • Purchase Information: When you make in-app purchases or subscribe, transaction data is processed through Apple's App Store (we don't store your payment details).
  • Support Communications (optional): If you contact our support team, we process the contact details you share (e.g., your email) solely to respond to your request.

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Analytics Data: We collect app usage analytics to improve the app experience. This includes:
    • App events (screen views, button taps, feature usage)
    • Error events (for debugging and stability improvements)
    • Purchase and subscription events (for service management)
    • Frame extraction events (count and operation type, not content)
    Analytics data is sent to our analytics backend and is associated with a pseudonymous user identifier (UUID or account email). This data is not used for advertising or cross-app tracking.
  • Device Information: We collect device type, iOS version, and app version. We use your device's identifierForVendor (a device-specific UUID that resets if you delete all apps from the developer) to generate your account identifier. This identifier is not shared with third parties and is only used for account management.
  • Credits Usage: We track your frame extraction credits, including monthly quota and add-on purchases, to manage your subscription and balance.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Video Content: We do not access, store, or upload your videos to our servers.
  • Extracted Frames: All frames are processed and saved locally on your device.
  • Photo Library: We only access videos with your explicit permission and never upload them.
  • Location Data: We do not collect your location information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Service Delivery: To provide and maintain the Fliqer app functionality.
  • Credits Management: To track and manage your frame extraction credits and subscriptions.
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app features.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide assistance.
  • Transaction Processing: To process purchases and manage subscriptions.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Processing

Fliqer processes all video content locally on your device. Your videos and extracted frames never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share them.

4.2 Cloud Storage

We store the following data on secure cloud servers:

  • User Account Data: Pseudonymous account identifier (user_[UUID]@fliq.app), authentication tokens, and account recovery information
  • Credits and Subscriptions: Credits balance, transaction history, subscription status, and purchase confirmations
  • Analytics Data: App usage events, error logs, and performance metrics (stored with pseudonymous user identifiers)
  • App Preferences: User settings and preferences synced across devices (if applicable)

4.3 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data
  • Secure authentication (JWT tokens)
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and monitoring

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For payment processing and subscription management. Apple processes your payment information according to their privacy policy.
  • Cloud Hosting: For storing account data, transaction history, and analytics data (all encrypted).
  • Analytics Backend: We operate our own analytics service (hosted on Vercel) to collect app usage data. This service processes analytics events with pseudonymous identifiers and does not share data with third-party analytics providers.

5.2 We Do NOT Share

  • Your video content or extracted frames
  • Personal information with advertisers (we have no ads)
  • Data with social media platforms
  • Information for marketing purposes

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or government request.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable data collection (may limit functionality)

6.2 Photo Library Access

You can manage photo library permissions in iOS Settings > Fliqer > Photos. Denying access will prevent the app from accessing your videos.

6.3 Subscription Management

Manage your subscription through the App Store. We cannot modify subscriptions directly.

7. Children's Privacy

Fliqer is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.
