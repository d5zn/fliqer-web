Your Privacy Matters
Fliqer is committed to protecting your privacy. We process video content locally on your device and never upload your videos.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data Collection

Information You Provide

  • Account Identifier: We use a pseudonymous identifier (`user_[UUID]@fliq.app`) for account management. We do not collect your real email unless you contact support.
  • Support Info: If you email us, we use your email only to reply.

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Analytics: Usage data (screen views, button taps) to improve the app. This is not linked to your personal identity.
  • Device Info: Device model and iOS version for debugging.
  • Credits: We track your credit balance to manage subscriptions.
What We DO NOT Collect:
We do not access, store, or upload your video content or extracted frames. All processing happens locally on your iPhone.

3. Data Usage

We use data to:

  • Provide app functionality
  • Manage your credits and subscriptions
  • Fix bugs and improve performance

4. Third-Party Services

We use Apple App Store for payments. We do not share data with advertisers or social networks.
