2. Description of Service

Fliqer is a professional iOS application that allows users to:

  • Browse videos from their photo library
  • Extract individual frames from videos with high precision
  • Apply AI enhancements to extracted frames (normal enhancement, blur fix, noise clean)
  • Save extracted frames in multiple formats (JPG, PNG, HEIC)
  • Choose save location (dedicated Fliqer album or directly to Recents)
  • Manage frame extraction credits and subscriptions
  • Sync credits and account across devices using Apple Sign In (optional)

Important: All video processing and frame extraction happens locally on your device. Your videos and extracted frames never leave your iPhone.
