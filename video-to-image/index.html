How to Convert Video to Image

Fliqer lets you convert any video to an image in seconds — without uploading your file anywhere. The entire conversion happens locally in your browser, so your videos stay completely private.

Common use cases for video to image conversion: creating thumbnails for YouTube or social media, saving a reference frame for design or illustration, capturing a moment from a screen recording, storyboarding, and motion analysis.

Steps to Convert Video to Image

  1. Drop or select your video — MP4, MOV, WebM supported
  2. Scrub the timeline to find the exact frame you want
  3. Click Capture to extract the frame as an image
  4. Download as full-resolution PNG or JPEG

FAQ

How do I convert a video to an image online?

Open the Fliqer video to image tool in your browser, load your video file, scrub to the frame you want, and click Capture. The image downloads instantly as a full-resolution PNG or JPEG — no account or upload required.

What image formats can I save from video?

The online tool exports PNG and JPEG. The iOS app also supports HEIC for efficient file sizes. All formats are exported in the original video resolution.

Does converting video to image upload my file?

No. Fliqer converts video to image entirely inside your browser using local processing. Your video never leaves your device — it stays completely private.

Can I extract multiple images from one video?

Yes. Use the step controls to navigate through the video and capture as many frames as you need. Each frame is downloaded as a separate image file.

Does the converted image keep the original resolution?

Yes. Fliqer extracts frames at the native resolution of your video. If your video is 4K, the extracted image will also be 4K.

Is the video to image converter free?

Yes. The Fliqer online video to image converter is completely free with no signup required. The iOS app includes a free tier with 30 credits per month.
