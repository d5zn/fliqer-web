User Guide

Extracting Frames

  1. Open an Album: Browse your video albums from the main screen
  2. Select a Video: Tap on any video to open it
  3. Navigate Time: Use the time slider to find the perfect moment
  4. Extract Frame: Tap the camera button to capture the frame
  5. Find Extracted Frames: Frames are saved to your photo library in the Fliqer album (or general library based on settings)

Navigation Tips

  • Swipe Left/Right: Navigate between videos in an album
  • Pin Albums: Swipe left on an album and tap the pin icon
  • Search: Use the search bar to quickly find albums
  • Time Precision: Tap on the time slider for frame-accurate positioning

Settings & Customization

  • Theme: Choose between Light, Dark, or System theme
  • Image Format: Select JPG, PNG, or HEIC format
  • Save Location: Choose Fliqer album or general library
  • Credits: View balance and manage subscriptions

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Where are my extracted frames saved?

By default, frames are saved to a dedicated "Fliqer" album in your photo library. You can change this to the general library in Settings.

Q: What video formats are supported?

Fliqer supports all video formats that iOS supports, including MP4, MOV, HEVC, and more.

Q: Can I extract frames from videos not in my library?

Currently, Fliqer only works with videos in your photo library. You'll need to save videos to your library first.

Q: How do credits reset?

Monthly subscription credits reset on your billing date. Add-on credits never expire and roll over indefinitely.

Q: What happens if I run out of credits?

You'll be prompted to either upgrade your subscription or purchase an add-on pack. You can also wait for your monthly credits to reset.

Q: Can I get a refund?

Refund requests must be made through Apple's App Store. We cannot process refunds directly. Go to reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund.

Q: Does Fliqer upload my videos?

No! All video processing happens locally on your device. We never upload or access your videos.

Q: Why can't I see any videos?

Make sure you've granted Fliqer access to your photo library. Go to Settings > Fliqer > Photos and select "All Photos."

Q: What image quality does Fliqer extract?

Fliqer extracts frames at the maximum quality available in the source video, preserving the original resolution.

Q: Can I restore my purchases on a new device?

Yes! Go to Settings in Fliqer and tap "Restore Purchases" to restore your subscriptions and add-on packs.

Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Force quit the app and reopen it
  • Restart your iPhone
  • Update to the latest iOS version
  • Update Fliqer to the latest version
  • Free up storage space on your device

Can't See Videos

  • Check photo library permissions in Settings > Fliqer > Photos
  • Ensure you have videos in your photo library
  • Try closing and reopening the app

Frame Extraction Fails

  • Check if you have sufficient storage space
  • Verify you have available credits
  • Try extracting from a different video
  • Check photo library write permissions

Purchase Issues

  • Verify your Apple ID has a valid payment method
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try "Restore Purchases" in Settings
  • Sign out and back into App Store
  • Contact Apple Support for payment issues

Credits Not Updating

  • Pull down to refresh on the main screen
  • Check your internet connection
  • Restart the app
  • Tap "Restore Purchases" in Settings
