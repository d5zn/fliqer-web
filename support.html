Getting Started

1. Install

Download Fliqer from the App Store and launch the app.

2. Access

Allow access to your Photo Library to load your videos.

3. Extract

Select a video and tap the shutter button to save frames.

Common Questions

Where are my frames saved?

Frames are saved to your iOS Photo Library. You can choose to save them in a dedicated "Fliqer" album or directly to Recents in the app settings.

Does Fliqer upload my videos?

No. Fliqer processes everything locally on your device. Your videos never leave your phone, ensuring 100% privacy.

How do credits work?

Each extracted frame uses 1 credit. Free users get 30 credits/month. Pro users get more. Add-on packs never expire.

What if I find a bug?

Please email us at support@fliqer.app with your device model and iOS version. We appreciate your feedback!
